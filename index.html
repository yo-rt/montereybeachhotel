Welcome to 
The Monterey Beach Hotel, Lytham St Annes

The Monterey is a 32 bedroom family owned and run hotel which offers a warm and friendly atmosphere. 
We are situated on the beach front at North Promenade, St.Annes. Our location is ideal for all ages as we are a stroll away from the town centre and beach front leisure facilities which include a public swimming pool, pier, cafes, shops, a cinema, casino, paddling pool, putting green miles of golden sand, the bowling greens on Ashton Gardens and of course, the Championship Golf Course of Royal Lytham. The only thing we overlook is the Sea!
